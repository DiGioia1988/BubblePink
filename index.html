What is Hello Brandon?!
Hello Brandon is a Deaf-accessible smart assistant designed for smartwatches like the Apple Watch and others. It helps Deaf and hard-of-hearing users communicate, stay informed, and manage daily tasks — all with accessibility in mind.

Whether you’re hiking, working, or just relaxing, Hello Brandon is always with you — offering visual alerts, gesture commands, and even AI-powered sign language translation features (coming soon). It’s like having a smart friend right on your wrist, built to understand your needs and respect your language.
